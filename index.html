Game Pitch

Demon of the Strait is a horror time simulation game where the player leads a group of friendly, happy-go-lucky birthday party guests in surviving the night from a gang of blood-thirsty vampires.

Gameplay Loop

Players must survive an entire night (round) and urge NPCs to stay indoors while they are being lured out by the vampire AI. At the end of the round players are given a rating based on how many NPCs survive the night.
