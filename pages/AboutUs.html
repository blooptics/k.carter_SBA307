About Us

Fab9, an independent video game development team based in Detroit, is driven by a mission to harness the power of interactive storytelling to uplift and inspire their local community. The team focuses on creating engaging narratives and gameplay experiences that reflect the rich culture, resilience, and unique perspectives of the city's residents. By developing games that resonate deeply with the Detroit community and beyond, Fab9 aims to not only entertain, but also to foster a sense of pride, connection, and positive representation within the indie gaming landscape.
